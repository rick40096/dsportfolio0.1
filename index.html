My key areas of expertise.

Artificial Inteligents

I have a strong foundation in Artificial Intelligence (AI), understanding its core concepts and applications in various domains. Data Structure

I possess a solid grasp of Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA), which forms the backbone of efficient programming and problem-solving.
